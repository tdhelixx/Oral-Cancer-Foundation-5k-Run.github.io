Get Ready to Join the 5K Run

Thank you for being part of the fight against oral cancer. Whether you're running, walking, donating, or cheering from the sidelines, your support helps raise critical awareness and funds for life-saving education and screenings.

Steps to Register:

  1. Click the "Register Here" button below to complete your registration through our event portal.
  2. Provide your name, contact information, and shirt size.
  3. Watch for a confirmation email with event details.
  4. Join us at Terry Hershey Park on October 26, 2025 — ready to run, walk, or celebrate!
Register or Donate Here

Other Ways You Can Help:

The registration page provides additional details about ways you can get involved, including creating a personal fundraising page, making a direct donation, helping spread the word on social media, or simply showing up to cheer for participants. Whether you run, walk, volunteer, or donate, your support brings us closer to saving lives through early detection and education.

  • Fundraise: Create your own fundraising page and help us reach more people.
  • Donate: Even if you can't attend, you can still support the cause.
  • Spread the Word: Share the event on social media using #OralCancerWalk.
  • Cheer: Come out and support the runners and walkers on race day!

Every action makes a difference — together, we're bringing education, early detection, and hope to more people.
