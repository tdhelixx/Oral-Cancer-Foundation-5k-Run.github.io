What is it?

The Oral Cancer Foundation is an IRS registered nonprofit 501(c) 3 and public service charity designed for advancy and service and to promte change in the community.


One of the foundation's primary missions is to raise awareness about the importance of annual oral cancer screenings. This need has become increasingly urgent due to the rising incidence of oral cancer. This year alone, approximately 58,500 people in the U.S. will be newly diagnosed.
